Tips for Choosing Dinner

Match the Weather

On cold days, warm soups like Kimchi-jjigae or Doenjang-jjigae are perfect. On hot days, try something refreshing like sushi or cold noodles.

Consider Your Company

Eating alone? Go for a quick hamburger or tteokbokki. Dining with friends? Samgyeopsal or pizza are great for sharing.

Keep It Balanced

Had greasy food yesterday? Opt for lighter options like sushi or salad today. Try to balance your meals over the week.
